## TARGETING CRITERIA

**Primary ICP (Ideal Customer Profile):**
- Title: CISO, CTO, VP of AI/ML, Head of Enterprise Architecture
- Company: Fortune 500 in Finance, Healthcare, Legal, Insurance, Government Contractors

**Secondary ICP:**
- CISOs at mid-market companies ($250M–$1B revenue) in regulated industries
- CTOs actively building internal AI tooling on OpenAI/Anthropic APIs

## SEQUENCE LOGISTICS

### Recommended Tools
- **CRM:** HubSpot or Salesforce with sequence automation
- **Enrichment:** Apollo.io or Clay for ICP signal triggers
- **Tracking:** Link tracking on all CTAs (demo, booking link)
- **Deliverability:** Send from founder@ or a named AE address — never a generic sales@ alias

### Sequence Timing
```
Day 1:  Email 1 (The Hook) — send Tuesday or Wednesday, 8–9am recipient local time
Day 5:  Email 2 (The Value) — send Thursday, 7–8am recipient local time
Day 12: Email 3 (The Breakup) — send Tuesday, 6–7am recipient local time
Day 20: LinkedIn connection request (no message — let the note be the outreach)
Day 25: LinkedIn DM (3 sentences max — reference a specific post they made)
```

### Qualification Criteria (SQL Threshold)
A lead becomes a **Sales Qualified Lead** when they:
- Reply to any email (even a "not now")
- Click the demo link and watch > 60 seconds
- Book a calendar slot
